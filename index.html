Ph.D. candidate, Computer Science, HCIED (HCI Engineering & Design) Lab @ Texas A&M University.

Human-Computer Interaction, Hybrid and Digital Fabrication, Sustainability

My research intersects focus on making 3D printing accessible to non-experts through precise material control. I explore the integration of traditional craft techniques, with 3D printing to expand design possibilities and improve fabrication workflows. A key goal is promoting sustainable practices by enabling the reuse and repurposing of materials, fostering a circular approach to fabrication.
